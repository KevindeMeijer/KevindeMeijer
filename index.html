Art Style


My inspiration comes from a lot of things and also depends on the project ofcourse. I try to incorporate accessibility into my designs, and like the idea of making them futureproof.

During my career i found myself wrestling about designs and decided to look into them. I love pastels and also the colors Piet Mondriaan used in his paintings

UX / UI Design


In design I iterate through the following design principal

  1. EMPATHISE and learn about the correct target audience.
  2. DEFINE and construct a point of view based on the users needs
  3. IDEATE and come up with creative solutions and designs.
  4. PROTOTYPING, and build the ideas I like.
  5. TEST, evaluate and iterate my deliverables like:

    • Personas
    • Site maps
    • Use cases and scenarios
    • User flow
    • Sketches and Infographics
    • Wireframes
